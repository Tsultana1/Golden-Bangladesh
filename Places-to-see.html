Places to see for tourists

Ahsan Manzil is located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Ahsan Manzil was the official residential palace and seat of the Dhaka Nawab Family. Ahsan Manzil is one of the most significant architectural monuments of Bangladesh. It has been designated as a national museum. The palace Ahsan Manzil is divided into two parts: the eastern side and the western side. The eastern building with the dome is called the Rangmahal and the western side with the living rooms is called Andarmahal.
